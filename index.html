OUR GAMES

Purranoia

Available Now!

Purranoia is an action roguelike with a twist to the genre - you have no control over your character's movement. Instead you control a variety of powerful scientific inventions to protect poor cat Mousti from waves of enemies. Master the chaos, upgrade your arsenal, and keep Mousti safe in this innovative take on the roguelike formula.

ABOUT US

Eerie Veery is an independent game studio dedicated to creating unique, atmospheric experiences that blend mystery with playful innovation. Founded by passionate developers who believe in the power of indie games to tell unconventional stories and push creative boundaries.


We specialize in crafting games that leave players with a sense of wonder and discovery. Our approach combines hand-crafted art, compelling gameplay mechanics, and that special indie magic that makes each playthrough memorable.
